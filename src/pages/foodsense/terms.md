## Terms

This page applies to **FoodSense**, an Omnise app.

### Product scope
FoodSense helps you organize your own observations. It does not identify allergies, intolerances, or provide nutritional advice.

### No diagnosis or recommendations
FoodSense is provided for personal tracking and educational information. It does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment recommendations.

### User responsibility
You are responsible for decisions you make based on information you record or view in the app.
