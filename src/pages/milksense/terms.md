## Terms

This page applies to **MilkSense**, an Omnise app.

### Visual status labels (Level 1 / Level 2 / Level 3)
MilkSense displays visual status labels for organizational and educational purposes only.

- These labels are **not measurements**.
- MilkSense does **not** determine safety, suitability, or feeding decisions.
- MilkSense does **not** provide recommendations or timing guidance.
- Results are based on user-provided or user-confirmed input.

### No medical or safety determinations
MilkSense is not a medical device and does not replace professional judgment or guidance.
